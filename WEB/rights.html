Project Backdrop

This project tracks satellite trajectories, offering real-time orbital insights for research, education, and space policy.

Importance

Satellite tracking is critical for avoiding collisions, ensuring seamless communication, and advancing space research.

Necessity

With increasing space activities, precise tracking is essential for safe space navigation and operational efficiency.
